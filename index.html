Our Ethos

Where Tradition Meets Tomorrow

Founded in 1983, House of Buddha began as a small family venture built on passion, heritage, and a deep love for authentic Chinese cuisine. What started as a modest neighborhood eatery has grown into a beloved local landmark — a place where families gather, friends meet up, and new guests discover flavors prepared with over four decades of tradition.

For us, food is more than a meal — it’s a connection to culture, to family, and to community. Every dish we serve reflects recipes passed down through generations, prepared with the same care and attention that inspired our founders more than 40 years ago.

Where Cantonese Comfort Meets Szechuan Spice

Our culinary style brings together the delicate flavors of Cantonese cooking with the bold, aromatic heat of Szechuan dishes. This unique balance allows us to offer a wide range of flavors — from mild and savory classics to fiery, spice-forward favorites.

Whether you crave the comforting warmth of Cantonese noodles or the intense kick of Szechuan chilis, our menu offers something for every taste.
